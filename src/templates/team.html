How it all started

One of our brands Limeproxies had huge traffic but the inbound sales had lower response time, customers dropped off if their questions were not answered in real-time, there were too many steps to call with calendar scheduling and we had low conversion rates.

One weekend, we quickly hacked this widget where customers can get automated calls by simply putting their number on this widget. It increased our all conversion and sign-up rates in a week. Hence, we thought about building it, not just for us, but for everyone. Hence, Limecall was born to help everyone to empower your sales teams.
